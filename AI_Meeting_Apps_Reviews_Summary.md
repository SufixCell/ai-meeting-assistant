# AI Meeting & Note Apps - Common Reviews

## Otter.ai
**Likes**
- Accurate transcription in quiet meetings
- AI summaries
- Easy sharing
- Zoom integration

**Complaints**
- Errors with accents/background noise
- Failed recordings
- Meeting bot joins calls
- Billing/cancellation issues
- Slow support

**Requests**
- Better speaker identification
- Offline recording
- Multilingual support
- Easier exports
- Transparent pricing

## Fireflies.ai
**Likes**
- Excellent summaries
- Strong integrations
- Automation

**Complaints**
- Missed recordings
- Confusing pricing
- Login issues
- Chatbot-first support

**Requests**
- Better mobile app
- Clearer pricing
- Faster human support

## Fathom
**Likes**
- Generous free plan
- Quality summaries
- Simple UI

**Complaints**
- Limited mobile support
- Paid AI features
- Transcript corrections

**Requests**
- Mobile app
- API
- More integrations
- Custom summaries

## tl;dv
- Likes: Highlights, timestamps, searchable recordings.
- Complaints: Privacy concerns, limited free tier, complex UI.
- Requests: Better mobile app, faster processing, exports, offline mode.

## Coconote
- Likes: Lecture summaries, flashcards, quizzes.
- Complaints: Technical term errors, free limits, formatting issues.
- Requests: Handwriting/PDF support, offline mode, more quiz types, more languages.

## TurboLearn AI
- Likes: Fast notes, flashcards, quizzes.
- Complaints: Upload limits, transcript errors, slow processing.
- Requests: Better editing, faster processing, unlimited uploads.

## Notta
- Likes: Multilingual support, accurate transcription, exports.
- Complaints: Speaker ID issues, expensive plans, editing friction.
- Requests: Offline transcription, better speaker detection, lower pricing.

## PLAUD AI
- Likes: High-quality recorder, long battery life, AI summaries.
- Complaints: Expensive hardware, AI credit limits, sync issues.
- Requests: More free AI processing, offline transcription.

## AudioPen
- Likes: Simple interface, brainstorming.
- Complaints: Limited editing, long recordings unsupported, expensive.
- Requests: Better exports, AI rewriting, folders.

# Common Complaints
- Recording failures
- Inaccurate transcription
- Expensive subscriptions
- Restrictive free plans
- Slow customer support
- No offline mode
- Speaker identification errors
- Limited export options

# Most Requested Features
1. Offline recording and transcription
2. Better transcription accuracy
3. More reliable recording
4. Lower pricing / better free tier
5. Better speaker identification
6. Faster AI summaries
7. More export formats
8. Human customer support
9. No mandatory meeting bot
10. Better multilingual support
